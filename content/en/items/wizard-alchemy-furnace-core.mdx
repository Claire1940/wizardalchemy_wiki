### Step-by-Step Dwarf King Farming

Farming the Dwarf King effectively can yield a substantial amount of **Furnace Cores**. This method combines a quest reward with repeatable boss drops.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate the Quest">
    Begin the quest associated with the **Wolf King**. This quest line will eventually lead you to the **Dwarf King**, offering a guaranteed **Furnace Core** upon completion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Dwarf King">
    Travel to the **Dwarf King's** lair. The boss is typically found in a designated area on the map. Prepare for a challenging fight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Engage and Exploit AI">
    Engage the **Dwarf King**. A common tactic involves drawing him to a specific rock or corner where his pathfinding can be exploited, allowing for safer attacks. Use your abilities and strong potions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Collect Rewards">
    Upon defeating the **Dwarf King**, you will receive the quest reward (**Furnace Core**) and have a chance to get 1-3 additional **Furnace Cores** from the boss drop itself.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Repeat for Efficiency">
    Once your build is strong enough to consistently defeat the **Dwarf King**, repeat the process. Consistent runs are key to accumulating a large number of **Furnace Cores**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip">
When fighting the Dwarf King, try to get into the center, use an ability, and then quickly run behind a large rock. He might follow, allowing you to attack from a safer position. If he doesn't follow, reset and try again.
</Callout>
