<Section icon={Target} title="How to Obtain Copper Earrings: Quests and Drops">

There are two primary ways to secure a **wizard alchemy copper earring**. For beginners, the most reliable method is through a specific NPC quest. For veteran players looking to mass-produce potions, farming specific enemy types is the only sustainable path.

### The Archer Goblin Quest
Upon entering the game, locate the quest-giving NPC near the central hub. This character offers a straightforward task that provides an immediate boost to your material inventory.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Accept the Quest">
    Locate the NPC near the shop area. Accept the quest to defeat **3 Archer Goblins**. This is a one-time high-value reward for new players.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Archer Goblins">
    Travel to the forested area past the initial spawn. Archer Goblins are typically found in small clusters, often guarded by Knife Goblins.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defeat the Targets">
    Use ranged spells to take them down. Be careful of their arrows, as they can stun you briefly, leaving you open to melee attacks from other mobs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Claim Your Reward">
    Return to the NPC to receive **2 Copper Earrings**. These are best saved for high-level recipes rather than wasted on early-game potions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Enemy Drop Rates
If you have already completed the quest, you will need to rely on RNG drops. Different enemies have significantly different probabilities of dropping the earring.

| Enemy Type | Drop Chance | Magic Power Provided | Difficulty |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Archer Goblin** | 13% - 35% | 30 | Low |
| **Mutant Archer Goblin** | 43% | 30 | Medium |
| **Mutant Warhammer Dwarf** | 43% | 30 | High |
| **Dwarf King Boss** | 43% | 30 | Boss |

<Callout type="warning" title="Combat Warning">
Do not attempt to farm Mutant variants or the Dwarf King until you have upgraded your wand. Standard Archer Goblins are the safest bet for consistent farming at lower levels.
</Callout>

</Section>

To farm the **wizard alchemy copper earring** effectively, you must understand where these enemies spawn and how they behave in combat. Archer Goblins are the most common source, but they are not the only ones.

When fighting Archer Goblins, the key is movement. They fire in a straight line, so "strafing" or moving perpendicular to their line of sight is the best defense. Since they have a 35% drop chance according to community testing, you should expect an earring roughly every three kills. If you are farming with a partner, the speed of these kills increases exponentially, making the "slow movement speed" of the game less of a hindrance.

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Boost">
Farming in a group of two is the "Gold Standard" for Copper Earrings. One player can distract the Knife Goblins while the other snipes the Archer Goblins from a distance.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Using Copper Earrings in Alchemy">

Once you have secured a stack of earrings, it is time to use them in the brewing menu. In Wizard Alchemy, every material has a Magic Score. The **wizard alchemy copper earring** contributes **30 points**, which is massive compared to the Blueberry's 3 points.

### Potion Recipe Requirements
To craft a specific potion, the sum of your materials' Magic Scores must reach a certain threshold. Overshooting the threshold by too much can actually lower your chances for a specific potion by qualifying you for a higher-tier one instead.

| Potion Name | Required Magic Value | Optimal Materials |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Frost Thorns** | 80 Magic | 2 Earrings + 4 Furnace Cores |
| **Lotus Bloom** | 100 Magic | 3 Earrings + High Power Items |
| **Radiant Sword** | 105 Magic | 3 Earrings + Dwarf Emblems |
| **Night Wraith** | 129 Magic | Earrings used as fillers |

### Material Power Comparison
To understand why the Copper Earring is so valuable, compare it to other common and rare materials available in the game.

| Material | Magic Power | Acquisition Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Blueberry | 3 | Bush / Chests |
| Seagull Egg | 5 | Nests / Chests |
| Dwarf Emblem | 12 | Dwarf Mobs |
| **Copper Earring** | **30** | **Archer Goblins** |
| Furnace Core | 43 | Bosses / Mutants |

<Callout type="info" title="Alchemy Mechanics">
Remember that crafting isn't just about the Magic Score. Your accuracy during the **Quick-Time Events (QTE)** determines the **Purity** of the potion, which directly impacts the damage output and cooldown of the resulting spell.
</Callout>
