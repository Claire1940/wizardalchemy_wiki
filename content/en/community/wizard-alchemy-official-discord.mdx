*   **#general-chat:** A place for casual conversation, sharing game experiences, and connecting with other players.
*   **#bug-reports:** Crucial for helping developers identify and fix issues. If you encounter any glitches or unexpected behavior in *Wizard Alchemy*, this is the place to report them.
