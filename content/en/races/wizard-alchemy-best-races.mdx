## Mastering Alchemy: Shards and Magic Power

The core of the game lies in its name: Alchemy. Even with the **wizard alchemy best races**, you still need to craft powerful potions to succeed. Crafting can be confusing, but the secret lies in understanding two main components: Total Magic Power and Elemental Shards.

### Increasing Magic Power
Total Magic Power determines the quality of the spell you receive from a potion. The higher your magic power, the better the spell. Currently, **Furnace Cores** are recognized as the material with the highest magic power contribution. By packing your recipes with high-power materials, you increase your chances of rolling an S-tier potion.

### Utilizing Elemental Shards
Elemental Shards are used to give your potions a specific "type." Adding an Elemental Shard gives you a 50% boost to that specific potion type. 
*   **Dark Element:** Currently the strongest element in the game. It is the only element capable of producing legendary potions that can one-shot high-level bosses like the Dwarf King.
*   **Ice Element:** Highly effective when paired with the Crystal race, offering great utility and damage.
*   **Fire Element:** A solid choice for consistent damage, often found in chests around the map.

## Farming Materials and Gold Efficiency

To sustain your alchemy habit and afford the 20,000 gold required for high-tier wands, you need a solid farming strategy. Gold is the lifeblood of progression in Wizard Alchemy, allowing you to buy better robes, staves, and even "Ascend" to increase your level cap and XP gain.

### The Dwarf King Strategy
The **Dwarf King** is widely regarded as the best boss for farming gold. While he can be intimidating, using a Dark-element legendary potion can make the fight trivial. Farming this boss repeatedly is the fastest way to accumulate the wealth needed for endgame equipment.

### Material Drop Rates
If you are hunting for specific materials, the Index is your best friend. For example:

*   **Pickaxe Dwarf:** The primary source for Dwarf Emblems and Golden Teeth.
*   **Mutant Enemies:** Located at the top of the map near the Dwarf King, these elite versions drop much rarer materials necessary for S-tier crafting.

## Combat Mechanics: The Art of the Parry

No matter how powerful your race or potion is, you will eventually face enemies that can deplete your health in seconds. This is where the **Parry** mechanic becomes essential. In Wizard Alchemy, a red indicator will appear when an enemy is about to launch a heavy attack. By pressing **'F'** at the exact moment the indicator flashes, you can parry the attack, nullifying damage and opening the enemy up for a counter-attack.

Mastering the parry is particularly important when fighting the Dwarf King or the Mutant Warhammer Dwarf. For your stat points, most veteran players suggest dumping everything into **Attack** and **Cooldown Reduction**. The logic is simple: if you can one-tap an enemy and dodge their attacks through parrying, you don't need to waste points on Health. This "glass cannon" approach is the most efficient way to clear content quickly.
