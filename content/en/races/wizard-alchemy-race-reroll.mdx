## Wizard Alchemy Race Tier List: Which Races to Aim For

Choosing the right race can make a significant difference in your *Wizard Alchemy* adventure. Here’s a breakdown of the top races, categorized by their rarity and overall utility:

### S-Tier: Best Overall DPS

*   **Thestrals (Legendary 1%)**: The undisputed king for damage output. Thestrals not only provide an exclusive attack effect that applies a 5-second "Disaster" damage-over-time effect but also boost Dark Elemental Damage, Attack Power, Movement Speed, Jump Height, and Skill Speed. If you roll this, keep it immediately; it's the ultimate target for story progression and bossing.

### A-Tier: Strong Progression Targets

*   **Stellar Ambassador (Epic 4%)**: An excellent all-rounder, offering a burst of movement speed after dealing damage, +20 Skill Speed, and +10% Attack Power. This race is flexible and powerful for general spell use and boss farming, making it a fantastic choice if you want a balanced progression.
*   **Death Eater (Rare 10%)**: Ideal for solo players due to its sustain capabilities. It restores 5% Max HP after killing an enemy (though HP regeneration speed is halved) and provides +10% Attack Power. This is a solid choice for farming and general progression, especially if you prefer not to specialize in Fire or Ice spells.

### B-Tier: Element-Specific Damage

*   **Fiendish Demon (Epic 5%)**: Perfect for Fire spell users. It grants +20% Fire Elemental Damage and +2 Base Movement Speed, but comes with a -10% Max HP penalty. Keep this if you're committed to a Fire build or enjoy faster kiting.
*   **Ice Crystal (Epic 5%)**: The counterpart to Fiendish Demon, this race is best for Ice spell users. It provides +20% Ice Elemental Damage and +10% Max HP, but reduces Base Movement Speed by -2. A great option for a tankier Ice elemental build.

### C-Tier: Good Early Upgrades

*   **Werewolf (Rare 10%)**: A solid early upgrade from Human. It offers a balanced mix of +10% Max HP, +5 Attack Power, +1 Base Movement Speed, and +10 Jump Height. While not top-tier, it’s a significant improvement and worth keeping until you accumulate more rerolls for A or S-tier targets.

### D-Tier: Replace Later

*   **Undead (Rare 10%)**: Offers a revive effect (leaves you at 1 HP, grants short invincibility, restores 30% Max HP, 180-second cooldown) and +10 Skill Speed, but with a hefty -30% Max HP penalty. Use it temporarily if you have no better options, but aim to replace it.
*   **Tree Spirit (Uncommon 15%)**: Provides x2 Life Regeneration and +5% Max HP, but reduces Base Movement Speed by -2. Useful at the very start for sustain but has weak long-term scaling.
*   **Elf (Uncommon 15%)**: A mobility-focused race with +3 Base Movement Speed and +10 Jump Height, but no direct damage or HP boost. Replace this once you can roll for Werewolf or better.
*   **Human (Starting Race / Common 25%)**: The default race with no stat bonuses. Always reroll out of Human as soon as you have the opportunity.
