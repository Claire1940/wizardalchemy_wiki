### Mastering Elemental Potions and Materials

Beyond your chosen race, understanding the alchemy system is paramount. Crafting powerful potions requires specific materials and elemental shards. The highest magic power material you can acquire is **Furnace Cores**. These are foundational for creating potent spells. Additionally, **elemental shards** are crucial, as they provide a 50% boost to a specific element type in your potion.

Currently, the **Dark type** is the most powerful element. It's the only element with a legendary potion and can even one-shot the formidable Dwarf King, making it incredibly efficient for farming gold. When crafting, combine high magic power materials with the appropriate elemental shard. For instance, adding a Fire Shard to your concoction gives it a 50% chance to gain a fire element. The more total magic power you infuse into a potion, the higher your chances of getting a better spell.

Materials can be obtained in several ways:
*   **Enemy Drops:** Different enemies drop specific materials. For example, Knife Goblins yield Goblin Fingers (75% chance) and Goblin Bones (25% chance). Pickaxe Dwarves drop Dwarf Emblems and Golden Teeth.
*   **Map Exploration:** Materials are scattered across the map.
*   **Chests:** Various chests found throughout the world offer valuable materials, including elemental shards like Fire Shards. Magic chests respawn after several hours, so revisit known locations.

Remember to refine your potions to make them even more overpowered. This often involves specific crafting stations or processes that enhance existing potions to S-tier or higher.

### Optimal Stat Allocation and Combat Mechanics

Your character's stats play a significant role in your combat effectiveness. While health might seem appealing, especially for beginners, the consensus among experienced players is to prioritize **Attack** and **Cooldown Reduction**. By maximizing your attack, you can often one-shot enemies, negating the need for excessive health. Cooldown reduction ensures your powerful spells are ready more frequently, maximizing your damage per second (DPS).

**Key Combat Tips:**
*   **Parrying:** Learning to parry is a vital skill, especially against stronger enemies like the Dwarf King or elite archers. A red indicator signals when to parry (by pressing F). Mastering this timing can drastically reduce incoming damage and open up counter-attack opportunities.
*   **Quests:** Always complete your quests. They are an excellent source of money, which is essential for progression, ascending, and acquiring better equipment.
*   **Ascension:** Don't neglect ascension! This crucial mechanic increases your level cap, boosts XP gain, and accelerates overall progress. It's a fundamental aspect of long-term character development in Wizard Alchemy.
