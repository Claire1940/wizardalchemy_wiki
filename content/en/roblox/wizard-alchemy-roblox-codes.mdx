## Master the Art of Potion Crafting

Crafting is the heart of Wizard Alchemy. Understanding how materials interact with your "Total Magic Power" is the key to unlocking S-tier potions and legendary spells. Many players find the crafting menu confusing at first, but it follows a logical progression based on the rarity and type of materials you use.

### Understanding Magic Power and Materials

Every material you add to your cauldron contributes to the Total Magic Power of the resulting potion. Higher magic power increases your chances of rolling a better spell. Based on community testing, **Furnace Cores** are currently the highest magic power materials available. If you want to craft the strongest possible spells, farming these should be your priority.

In addition to raw power, you can use **Element Shards** to influence the type of potion you create. Adding a shard gives you a 50% boost toward a specific element, such as:

*   **Fire Shards:** Used for fire-based destruction.
*   **Ice Shards:** Essential for freezing and slowing enemies.
*   **Dark Shards:** Currently considered the best element in the game.

According to veteran players, the Dark element is the only one that currently features a Legendary potion. This potion is so powerful that it can reportedly one-shot the Dwarf King, the game’s primary gold-farming boss.

### Refining Your Potions

Don't settle for a basic brew. The game allows you to refine your potions to make them even more overpowered. Look for refining stations near major hubs or the lighthouse to upgrade your A-tier potions into S-tier masterpieces. This process is essential for end-game progression and tackling elite enemies.

## Combat Mechanics: Parrying and Stats

While alchemy is the name of the game, you won't survive long without mastering the combat system. Unlike many other Roblox RPGs, Wizard Alchemy requires active engagement and timing.

### The Importance of Parrying

One of the most overlooked features is the **Parry** mechanic. When an enemy is about to land a heavy hit, a red indicator will appear. Pressing the **F key** at the exact moment of the indicator allows you to parry the attack, nullifying damage and often stunning the opponent. Learning to parry is essential for defeating high-level bosses like the Dwarf King, who can otherwise deplete your health in seconds.

### Stat Allocation Strategy

Every time you level up, you earn stat points. While it might be tempting to build a tanky character, the community consensus is that **Attack** and **Cooldown Reduction (CDR)** are the most important stats. By maximizing your DPS, you can "one-tap" common enemies, meaning you don't need to worry about your health pool if the enemy never gets a chance to strike. Only invest in health if you find yourself struggling with the timing of parries.
