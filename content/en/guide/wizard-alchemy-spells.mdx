<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Alchemy & Brewing System">

The heart of the experience lies in the Alchemy Table. To obtain high-tier **wizard alchemy spells**, you must understand the relationship between **Magic Score** and **RNG**. Every material has a hidden or visible magic value; the sum of these values determines which potion you are likely to produce.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Material Selection">
    Place up to five materials into the cauldron. Each material contributes to the total Magic Score. For example, a Blueberry provides 3 Magic, while a Seagull Egg provides 5.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Brewing Minigame">
    Complete three phases: **Grind** (no penalty for mistakes), **Pour** (hold until the meter hits the sweet spot), and **Stir** (click overlapping rings).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Purity & Rarity">
    Your accuracy in the minigame determines the **Purity** percentage. A 90%+ Purity result can turn an A-tier potion into an S-tier version with significantly higher stats.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Overshooting Values">
If you exceed the required Magic Score for a specific potion, your percentage chance to craft that potion actually decreases as you begin qualifying for the next tier of spells. Always aim for the exact threshold for the best odds.
</Callout>
