## Understanding the Core Mechanics of Wizard Alchemy

At its heart, *Wizard Alchemy* is an RPG where progression hinges on mastering various interconnected systems. ## Gearing Up and Mastering Your Stats

Your wizard's effectiveness isn't solely dependent on spells; equipment and smart stat allocation play equally crucial roles. The game features a shop where you can purchase various gear:

*   **Wands:** These are your primary damage dealers. Upgrading your wand directly translates to higher attack power, making battles easier and faster.
*   **Wizard Hats and Robes:** These defensive pieces of equipment boost your health, increasing your survivability in challenging encounters.

Beyond equipment, managing your character's stats is vital. Every time you level up, you earn stat points that can be invested into four core attributes:

*   **Attack:** Increases the damage of your normal attacks and abilities.
*   **HP:** Boosts your overall health, making you more durable against enemy attacks.
*   **Cooldown Reduction:** Allows you to use your magical abilities more frequently, improving your combat flow.
*   **Movement Speed:** Determines how quickly your character moves across the map, essential for dodging attacks and efficient questing.

A fantastic feature in *Wizard Alchemy* is the ability to reset your stat points for free at any time. This encourages experimentation, allowing you to try different builds without penalty until you find one that perfectly suits your playstyle. Whether you prefer a glass cannon mage or a sturdy battle wizard, you can tailor your stats accordingly.
