## Mastering the Art of Potion Crafting and Material Gathering

Potion crafting is the heart of *Wizard Alchemy*, allowing you to create powerful spells and buffs. Understanding which materials to use and how to acquire them is key to success.

### Key Materials and Their Uses

*   **Furnace Cores:** These are your go-to for maximizing magic power in your potions. The more Furnace Cores you add, the higher the total magic power, which directly increases your chance of getting a better spell.
*   **Elemental Shards:** These shards, like Ice Shards or Fire Shards, provide a 50% boost to a specific element type in your potion. For example, adding an Ice Shard gives your potion an ice element.
*   **Best Element Type:** Currently, the Dark element is considered the most powerful, as it's the only one with a legendary potion and can even one-shot the formidable Dwarf King.

### Where to Find Materials

Materials can be acquired in several ways:

*   **Enemy Drops:** Every enemy has a chance to drop specific materials. For instance, a Knife Goblin might drop Goblin Fingers (75% chance) or Goblin Bones (25% chance). The Pickaxe Dwarf is known to drop Dwarf Emblems or Golden Teeth. Check your in-game index to see what each enemy drops.
*   **Map Exploration:** Valuable materials are scattered throughout the map. Keep an eye out for glowing spots and environmental interactions.
*   **Elite Enemies:** Stronger, elite enemies like the Mutant Archer Goblin (found above the Dwarf King's location) and the Mutant Warhammer Dwarf offer rarer materials.
*   **Big Bird Nest:** Located at the very top of the map, breaking this nest (which requires significant damage) can yield crazy materials for powerful potions.

### Crafting High-Tier Potions

To create a potent potion, prioritize materials that offer high magic power (like Furnace Cores). Then, strategically add an elemental shard to imbue it with a specific element. For example, combining high magic power materials with a Fire Shard gives you a 50% chance to create a fire-element potion. Don't forget to refine your potions to make them even more overpowered – look for the refining station, often found near important landmarks.

## Maximizing Your Character: Stats, Ascensions, and Combat Mastery

Beyond crafting, optimizing your character's stats and combat skills is crucial for tackling stronger enemies and progressing through *Wizard Alchemy*.

### Stat Allocation

When leveling up, you gain stat points. While health can be tempting, focusing on **Attack** and **Cooldown Reduction** is generally the most effective strategy. Maximizing attack allows you to quickly defeat enemies, often before they can deal significant damage, effectively negating the need for excessive health. Cooldown reduction ensures your spells are ready more frequently, maximizing your DPS (damage per second). This combination helps you one-shot weaker enemies and deal heavy damage to bosses.

### Ascension

Ascending is a critical mechanic that increases your level cap, improves XP gain, and generally speeds up your overall progression. Don't neglect this feature! If you're not ascending regularly, you're missing out on faster growth and power spikes. Ensure you spend your hard-earned money to ascend as soon as possible.

### Combat Techniques: The Art of Parry

A vital combat skill often overlooked is parrying. When a red indicator appears on an enemy, it's your cue to parry by pressing `F`. Getting the timing right can be challenging initially, but mastering the parry is incredibly effective, especially against stronger foes like the Dwarf King and archers. Successful parries can turn the tide of difficult battles, allowing you to survive encounters that would otherwise be impossible and secure those rare materials from tougher enemies.

### Gold Income and Quests

Money is the lifeblood of progression in *Wizard Alchemy*. You need it for ascensions, better gear, and various upgrades.

*   **Beginner:** Focus on completing all available quests. Quests provide a consistent and reliable source of gold for new players.
*   **Mid-to-End Game:** The Dwarf King is your primary gold farming target. This boss drops significant amounts of gold and valuable materials, making it the best monster to farm for quick money.

Remember to always prioritize your gold income. Completing quests and farming the Dwarf King are the most efficient ways to ensure you have enough currency to progress.

### Wands and Equipment

While early game wands are easy to acquire, keep an eye out for hidden upgrades. For instance, an Ice Wizard Wand can be found inside a waterfall for 20,000 gold. The end-game wand, the Amber Staff, is located in the treehouse near the Dwarf King. Always seek out better equipment to enhance your magical abilities. Additionally, if you find yourself taking too much damage, consider acquiring wizard robes to increase your health.
