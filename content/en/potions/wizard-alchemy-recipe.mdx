### Basic Crafting Mechanics

| Mechanic          | Description                                                                     | Key Impact                                |
| :---------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | :---------------------------------------- |
| **Magic Score**   | Every material has a specific Magic Score (e.g., **Blueberry 3**).              | Determines the total Magic Value of the brew. |
| **Magic Value**   | Each potion requires a minimum Magic Value to be crafted (e.g., **Ice Spike 10**). | Meeting this unlocks the potion for crafting. |
| **Material Slots**| You have 5 slots in the Alchemy menu to combine materials.                      | Strategic placement and combination are key. |
| **Quick-Time Event**| A mini-game during brewing where accuracy affects potion **Purity**.            | **Purity** boosts spell stats like damage. |

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Essential Potion Recipes and Magic Requirements">
Crafting specific potions requires meeting their minimum Magic Value. This is achieved by combining materials whose individual Magic Scores add up to or exceed the potion's requirement. For high-end spells, incorporating Elemental Shards is crucial.

<Callout type="warning" title="Elemental Shard Usage">
Elemental Shards (Ice, Fire, Earth, Dark, etc.) do not have Magic Scores themselves but are vital for increasing the chances of crafting specific elemental spells. Always include the matching Elemental Shard for your desired spell! For example, use **Dark Shards** for the **Night Wraith Potion**.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering Potion Refinement for SSS Grade">
Refinement is a critical endgame process that allows you to amplify the power of your spells. By combining multiple instances of the same potion, you can upgrade its grade, significantly boosting your magic power, spell damage, and cast range. This process is essential for dominating the game.

<Callout type="success" title="Refinement Benefits">
Reaching **SSS grade** through refinement massively boosts your magic power, directly increasing your spell damage and cast range. This is often the difference between struggling against tough bosses and easily crushing them.
</Callout>

### The Refinement Process

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Refinement UI">
    Head to the right side of the alchemy table at the spawn point to open the Refinement UI. This is where all your refinement magic happens.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Potions for Refinement">
    Choose the potion you wish to refine. You will need multiple copies of the *exact same potion* to proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Combine and Upgrade">
    Combine 3 normal-grade potions to get one S-grade potion.
    Combine 4 S-grade potions to get one SS-grade potion.
    Combine 5 SS-grade potions to get one SSS-grade potion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Enjoy Enhanced Power">
    Once refined to SSS grade, your spell will have significantly increased damage and range, making it far more potent in combat.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
To achieve a guaranteed SSS Refinement for a single spell, you will need a total of **60 normal-grade potions** of that specific type. This highlights the importance of efficient farming for your chosen primary spells.
</Callout>
