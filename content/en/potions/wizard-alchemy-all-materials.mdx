## The Core Mechanics of Alchemy Materials

Before hunting for specific drops, it is crucial to understand how materials function within the game's UI. Every item you place into the cauldron contributes to a "Total Magic Power" stat. The higher this number, the better the quality of the resulting spell. However, simply stacking high-power items isn't always enough.

According to veteran players, the most sought-after material for pure power is the **Furnace Core**. While common items like Goblin Fingers provide a baseline, Furnace Cores act as the heavy hitters for endgame builds. Additionally, Elemental Shards (Fire, Ice, and Dark) do not just add power; they provide a 50% boost to the probability of your potion taking on that specific elemental affinity. Currently, the community consensus is that the **Dark Element** is the most powerful, as it is the only element capable of producing Legendary-tier potions that can trivialise boss fights.
